🧩 Play style segmentation

Play styles are assigned using a rule-based segmentation — a simplified form of supervised classification. The assignment is deterministic and hierarchical: Completionist → Speedrunner → Manipulator → Balanced (catch-all). This mirrors the IBM DS approach to feature-based clustering with explicit decision thresholds.
